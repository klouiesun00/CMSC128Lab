Book Shelf is a web application where you can keep track of all the books you've read, and the books you want to read in the future! Create your own profile to save your book list and interact with other users through the forums. Book Shelf is the first web application to offer a place where you can store your books and share your own book reviews. Signing up is easy: just click on the My Profile page, enter your details, and start sharing!

This web application was inspired by the designer's desire to be able to keep track of all the books she's read and to see what other readers like her have to say about popular novels.

The Tower of Nero by Rick Riordan

Review by Shannon

The Tower of Nero is the last installment of Riordan's series, The Trials of Apollo. I've been reading Riordan's books ever since I picked up "The Lightning Thief" 5 years ago, and he never disappoints. Apollo's charismatic personality was not lost in the gripping pages of the book and his character development was written so endearingly that you can't help but to cheer him on. This book wrapped up the series nicely, and I wouldn't expect anything less from Rick Riordan. 10/10!
